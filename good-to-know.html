The Franciscan Brand on the Web

Page last updated Oct 2, 2024

In 2024, Franciscan University received new brand guidelines that included new colors, fonts, logos, voice, tagline, and more. Many of the old colors and fonts are still in use, however, making the "new" brand guideline a mix of old and new. Brand elements that are or will become obsolete will be noted in this guide.
